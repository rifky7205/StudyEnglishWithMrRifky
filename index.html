What is Simple Present Tense?

The simple present tense in English is used to describe habits, repeated actions, facts, and general truths.

Formula: Subject + Verb 1 + Object + Adverb of Frequency

Examples:

Example 1: I study English every day.
Example 2: They go to school.
Example 3: We drink water.
